Fatemeh Bahrani

I am a CS Researcher at University of Southern California, working under the supervision of professor Erdem Bıyık and professor Heather Culbertson. I am interested in foundation models, robotics, and human-robot interaction. My research focuses on developing efficient and interpretable models that can adapt to dynamic environments and enhance the interactions between human and robot.
